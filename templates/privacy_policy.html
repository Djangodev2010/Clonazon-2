Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 26, 2026

At Clonazon, accessible from our modern shopping platform, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Clonazon and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

When you register for an Account (your Profile), we may ask for your contact information, including items such as name, company name, address, email address, and telephone number.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • • Provide, operate, and maintain our website
  • • Improve, personalize, and expand our store offerings
  • • Understand and analyze how you use our platform
  • • Develop new products (like more Phones or Furniture), services, features, and functionality
  • • Send you emails regarding your orders or our Newsletter

3. Log Files

Clonazon follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, and referring/exit pages.

4. Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, Clonazon uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited.

5. Data Protection Rights

We would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

  • The right to access – You have the right to request copies of your personal data.
  • The right to rectification – You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate.

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
